About us

Team
 
The team at Invenflow has distributed well over 500,000 products, acting as a trusted supplier to an entire network of trade buyers, ranging from sole traders to multi-store chains.
 
We scour the globe for the most competitive deals, so that you can focus on what matters, selling!
 
Our team of traders are always on hand to meet your needs, answer questions and help process any orders.
 
We have a dedicated team who run our seamless logistics operation, ensuring goods are dispatched by the box or pallet in a timely manner, right to your door.
